PGR2 FILES  

1. MAGIC ERASER -- Remove unwanted objects from photos.    

------------------------------------------------------------  

2. IN PAINT (BIG-LAMA) -- Remove parts of a photo & replace them with any other object.    

------------------------------------------------------------  

3. OUTPAINT -- Extends an image beyond its original borders while maintaining style.    

------------------------------------------------------------  

4. STYLE TRANSFER -- Applies the artistic style of one image to the content of another using AI.    

------------------------------------------------------------  

5. COLORIZE (DDCOLOR) -- Colorize a black & white photo.    

------------------------------------------------------------  

6. IMG GENERATION (realvisxlV50_v50, juggernautXL_v8Rundiffusion) -- Text-to-image generation using AI.    

------------------------------------------------------------  

7. GFPGAN (IMAGE ENHANCER) -- Enhance images (mostly portraits & faces).    

------------------------------------------------------------  

8. BACKGROUND BLUR (REMBG) -- Blur the background.    

------------------------------------------------------------  

9. BACKGROUND REMOVAL (REMBG) -- Remove the background.    

------------------------------------------------------------  

10. IMG TO PENCIL SKETCH (OPENCV) -- Convert an image into a pencil sketch.  

------------------------------------------------------------
